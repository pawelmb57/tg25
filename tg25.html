Turkey Strategy

Turkey Plan (Two Birds)

All legs sous vide; one bird for leftovers (SV breasts), one for showpiece roast.

Dark Meat (Both)

  • Season/bag all legs & thighs together (salt/pepper/aromatics).
  • Sous vide 155°F for 12–16 hrs; ice bath 20–30 min.
  • Friday: reheat 145°F bath 45 min; broil 3–5 min to crisp.

Turkey A (Leftovers)

  • Remove breasts; vac-seal separately with light seasoning.
  • Sous vide 145°F for 2.5–3 hrs; ice bath 20–30 min.
  • Refrigerate for sandwiches/leftovers.

Turkey B (Showpiece)

  • Spatchcock; dry brine Wed/Thu (about 1 tbsp kosher salt per 4 lb).
  • Fri: Herb Butter; roast 425°F to 160°F breast; rest 20 min.
  • Use drippings for gravy; serve breast at table.
Recipe

Creamy Coconut–Ginger Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs sweet potatoes, peeled + chunks
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt + drizzle neutral oil (bag)
  • 1 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 2–3 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp butter or coconut oil
  • 1 lime (zest + juice)
  • Maple syrup, salt, pepper to finish

Steps

  • Tue: Bag 3 lb chunks with 1/2 tsp salt + drizzle oil (optional ginger slices). SV 185°F 90 min. Ice bath 20–30 min; refrigerate.
  • Fri: Warm coconut milk + ginger + butter/oil + lime zest (elixir) until steaming.
  • Warm potatoes (in bag or pot), drain, mash with elixir; finish with lime juice + maple + salt/pepper.
Recipe

Seven-Vegetable Orchestra

Components

Group A: Carrots, Parsnips, Turnips

Group B: Squash, Fennel

Fresh: Red Onion, Radishes

Execution

Bag Tuesday: 1/2-inch pieces; each bag gets 1 tsp olive oil + 1/2 tsp salt (Group A add thyme). SV 185°F — A: 60–75m, B: 45–60m. Ice bath 20–30m.

Fri: Warm bags, drain. Broil with fresh onion/radish (squash/fennel 5–7m; roots 8–12m). Toss hot with brown butter + thyme + lemon zest.

Recipe

Mashed Potatoes

  • Prep (Tue): Peel/cut 8 lb potatoes; store in cold water.
  • Cook (Wed): Boil 15–20 min in salted water. Mash with butter, warm milk/cream, sour cream; chill covered.
  • Fri: Reheat gently; stir in 1/2–1 c warm dairy + 2–3 tbsp butter to loosen.
